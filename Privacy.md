# Privacy Policy for ColdEmailCopilot

*Last Updated: December 30, 2025*

## Overview

ColdEmailCopilot is a Chrome extension that helps users generate personalized cold emails from LinkedIn profiles using AI. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

1. **Google Account Information**
   - Email address (via Google OAuth)
   - Used for: Authentication and user identification

2. **LinkedIn Profile Data**
   - Publicly visible LinkedIn profile information you choose to process
   - Includes: Name, headline, experience, education, and other profile sections
   - Used for: AI-powered email generation

3. **Usage Statistics**
   - Number of emails generated
   - AI provider used (OpenAI or Anthropic)
   - Token usage (for billing/limits)
   - Used for: Usage tracking and spending limits

4. **User Preferences**
   - Email templates
   - Saved profiles
   - Tone preferences
   - AI model selection
   - Used for: Personalizing your experience

### What We DON'T Collect

- We do NOT access your Gmail inbox or sent emails
- We do NOT store your passwords
- We do NOT track your browsing activity outside of LinkedIn
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties

## Data Storage

All data is stored securely on:
- **Vercel Postgres (Neon)**: User profiles, preferences, and usage statistics
- **Your Browser**: Local extension settings via Chrome Storage API

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

## Data Usage

We use your data solely to:
1. Authenticate your account
2. Generate personalized emails using AI
3. Track usage to enforce fair-use limits
4. Improve the service

We do NOT use your data for:
- Marketing or advertising
- Training AI models (your data stays private)
- Sharing with third parties (except AI providers for processing)

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. **Google OAuth** - For authentication
2. **OpenAI** - For GPT-based email generation (optional)
3. **Anthropic** - For Claude-based email generation (optional)
4. **Vercel** - For hosting backend services and database

Each service has its own privacy policy. Your LinkedIn data is sent to AI providers only when you explicitly generate an email.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while you use the service
- **Generated Emails**: Not stored (only shown to you)
- **Usage Statistics**: Retained indefinitely for billing/analytics
- **LinkedIn Profile Data**: Cached temporarily, can be cleared anytime

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data stored in our database
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your preferences and templates
- **Opt-out** of data collection (by uninstalling the extension)
